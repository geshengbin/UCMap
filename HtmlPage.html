The Current State of Insecurity Defense in Doubt

Enterprises live in a constant state of insecurity, never knowing with any certainty if their networks have been compromised.

Undetected Attacks

Today’s advanced persistent threats bypass traditional security measures and go undetected for months, leaving enterprises unknowingly exposed and vulnerable to breaches.

Point-in-Time Solutions

Current solutions provide only a static snapshot of threats, rather than a dynamic view of their full lifecycle. This ‘look once’ approach is not effective against today's sophisticated, targeted attacks.

Siloed Intelligence

Retrieving and correlating information from a host of endpoint solutions is time consuming, expensive and often impossible, slowing incident response and providing only partial threat intelligence.

Enterprise is an Island

Organizations have no tools to collaborate or benefit from each other’s threat intelligence in a timely manner that could help stop attacks from proliferating.

a better path Security Enlightened

Platform Cloud Network DVR

The ProtectWise Cloud Network DVR is a virtual camera in the cloud that records all network traffic so that businesses can see everything, including events in the past, identify what matters most, and respond more quickly and effectively to threats.

Visibility

See Everything

  • Netflow, metadata, truncated flows and full-fidelity PCAP by protocol and application
  • Long-term retention allows for analysis of security events and observations now and in the past days, weeks, months and even years

Detection
